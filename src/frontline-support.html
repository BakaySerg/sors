SORS was built around facility support services and continues to provide unsurpassed excellence for Commercial, and government projects. Our latest ventures introduce innovative and disruptive technologies that change the way organizations operate.

At SORS, our commitment to constant improvement is what keeps us at the forefront of our industry. With the proliferation of cloud-based services, mobile devices, and e-commerce; more and more customers today encounter frustrating tech issues that demand rapid resolution.

SORS's Help Desk provides our Clients with a 24/7 comprehensive job logging service that operates 365 days of the year. Any query presented, at any time, it will be individually handled by a member of the team and reported back to you. We promise your query will be treated by our team with extreme care and attention, in order to ensure that operational services for your business are provided 24/7/365.

Often regarded as Tier 1 in the Service Desk model, customer support (or Help Desk) is the front line and the most important interface between you and your clients.

The main features of this support service include:

  1. Your queries are immediately referred from the Service directly to the appropriate operations team
  2. All communication is handled either by email or mobile
  3. Service Desk is operated by a live professional who is familiar with the services we provide to you and will forward any maintenance or repair issue, in real-time, to our on-call technicians
  4. All work is scheduled, either as Planned Preventative Maintenance (PPM) or reactive services through the Frontline Service Desk
  5. The personnel from our Service Desk are in regular communication with our customer account managers
  6. There is an escalation process so that all matters are appropriately and successfully handled
  7. Communication is always polite and efficient
  8. Our service extends to out of hours, and appropriate holiday and sickness cover is always in place
  9. One phone call to our Service Desk and our clients are assured that their issue is being handled – with feedback communication if necessary
SORS provides extremely cost-effective, early first contact resolution, world-class offshore Tier 1 to Tier 3 customer support as an extension (co-source) to your existing team or a completely outsourced alternative.
